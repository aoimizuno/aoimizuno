## 🛒 About Me

I'm an **AI Integration & E-Commerce Front-End Developer** with **7+ years of experience** building high-converting, intelligent online shopping experiences.

I specialize in embedding **LLMs, AI agents, and smart automation** into e-commerce platforms — from AI-powered product search and personalized recommendations to conversational shopping assistants and automated order workflows.

Over the years, I've designed and shipped:

- 🤖 AI-powered product recommendation engines, chatbots, and shopping copilots
- 🛍️ High-performance storefronts with React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS — optimized for conversion
- 🔍 Smart search & filtering systems using vector embeddings and semantic search
- ⚡ E-commerce integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, and headless commerce platforms
- 📊 AI-driven analytics dashboards for sales insights, customer behavior, and inventory forecasting
- 🔗 Full-stack SaaS platforms with real-time data pipelines and LLM-backed automation

I'm passionate about making **AI feel like a natural part of the shopping journey** — not a gimmick.
